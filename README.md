## Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end commercial analytics workflow developed to assess the potential financial impact of Australia's **RBA Surcharge Reform (effective 1 October 2026)** on a large merchant payments portfolio.

Using Python, I transformed raw merchant transaction data into actionable commercial insights, developed predictive pricing models, analysed merchant profitability under future pricing scenarios, and identified strategies to minimise margin loss and merchant churn.

> **Note:** This repository does **not** contain any confidential company data. All proprietary datasets, dashboards, presentations and business-sensitive information have been removed.

---

# Business Problem

The proposed surcharge reforms will prevent merchants from recovering card acceptance costs through customer surcharges.

The objective of this project was to answer key commercial questions such as:

- Which industries are most exposed?
- Which merchants are likely to become unprofitable?
- How much portfolio margin could be lost?
- Which merchants should be prioritised for repricing?
- What commercial strategies can reduce the impact?
